# PRODUCT

**Register:** product

## Product purpose

Orrery is a fully custom Hypixel SkyBlock client. "Observatory" is its launcher —
the home app that authenticates the player, manages game files, injects the mod,
runs the launch pipeline, and hosts the config / cosmetics UI. The launcher and
the in-game UI share one brand: this is not a Minecraft mod with a launcher bolted
on, it is one coherent instrument.

## Users

Hypixel SkyBlock players — game-literate, detail-oriented, data-hungry. They live
in dense menus and aligned numbers (coins, prices, stats). They expect a launcher
that feels precise and fast, not a toy. They will open this every session, so it
must reward familiarity and never feel gimmicky.

## Brand & tone

"Brass instrument in the void." A precision brass machine modelling the heavens:
deep cosmic void surfaces, warm brass/gold as the primary accent, cold starlight
cyan as the counterpoint, hairline orbital motifs throughout. Warm metal against
cold void. Precise, instrument-like, dark, restrained. The orbital-rings motif is
the signature (it literally is an orrery). Glow is layered low-opacity strokes and
concentric rings, never neon, never heavy blur. Restraint reads as "instrument";
excess reads as "hack client."

## Anti-references

- Vanilla Minecraft chrome: pixel fonts, dirt/stone textures, beveled buttons,
  the vanilla slot grid. Never.
- "Hack client" aesthetics: neon glow, heavy blur, RGB, aggressive gradients.
- Generic dark SaaS dashboard: flat grey cards, no identity, could-be-anything.
- Playful / blocky / cartoonish game-launcher styling.

## Strategic principles

- The custom typeface is the keystone — the moment UI stops using Minecraft's
  pixel font it stops reading as a mod.
- One brand across launcher and in-game UI; they cannot drift (shared tokens).
- Identity lives in the orbital motif, hairlines, brass-on-void, and the type.
  Apply it as ambient craft, not loud decoration.
- Earned familiarity over novelty: standard launcher patterns (side nav, hero
  play action, news rail, status footer), executed with exceptional finish.
